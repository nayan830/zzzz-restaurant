About Us

Where Flavours
Tell Stories

"Every plate at Banjara is a journey – from the smoky tandoors to the aromatic biryanis – crafted with love for every guest."

Located in the bustling Free School Street area near Kolkata's iconic New Market, Banjara Multi Cuisine Restaurant has carved a special place in the hearts of food lovers. Our name, meaning "wanderer," reflects our philosophy – a journey through the finest multi-cuisine flavours, all under one cozy, minimalistic roof.

From the first bite to the last, every dish is prepared with premium ingredients and recipes that honour both tradition and creativity. Whether you crave a royal Mughlai biryani, spicy Indo-Chinese starters, or creamy continental pasta – Banjara has it all.
